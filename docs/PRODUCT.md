# Welcome to Fee X-ray

Fee X-ray is your automated financial auditor. It connects directly to your business bank accounts and payment processors to find hidden fees, mistaken charges, and unused subscriptions, helping you put money back in your pocket.

## How It Works

1. **Sign Up & Connect**
   When you sign up, you'll create an Organization. You can invite your team members to join. Next, you securely link your bank accounts or payment processors (like Stripe or PayPal) using our banking partner, Plaid. 
   *(Note: We never see or store your bank login credentials).*

2. **The X-ray Scan**
   Once connected, our Analysis Engine goes to work. It syncs your recent transactions and runs them through our proprietary rules engine. It acts like a detective, looking for:
   - **Zombie Subscriptions**: Software you pay for but haven't used in months.
   - **Unwaived Bank Fees**: Courtesy fees (like overdrafts or wire fees) that banks will often waive if you just ask.
   - **Processor Markups**: Payment processing fees that are higher than the industry standard benchmark.
   - **Chargebacks**: Disputed customer payments that you need to respond to.

3. **The Dashboard**
   You receive a clean, easy-to-read dashboard showing exactly how much money you are losing to these hidden fees. We highlight the most expensive issues first, giving you plain-English instructions on how to resolve them.

4. **Continuous Monitoring (PRO Plan)**
   If you upgrade to our PRO plan, Fee X-ray doesn't just run once. It continuously monitors your accounts in the background, sending you alerts the moment a new hidden fee is detected.

## Privacy & Security

Your data is yours. 
- We use bank-grade encryption to store your connection details. 
- Our system is audited and monitored continuously.
- We only have "read-only" access to your transactions—we cannot move your money.
